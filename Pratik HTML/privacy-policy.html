Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or contact us, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Project-related details (type of project, budget range, location)
  • Communication preferences and any notes you provide

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your appointment requests and enquiries
  • To provide architecture and interior design services
  • To send project updates and relevant communications
  • To improve our website and services

3. WhatsApp Communication

Our appointment and contact forms send information directly via WhatsApp. By submitting these forms, you consent to your project details being shared through WhatsApp's platform. WhatsApp's own privacy policy governs the handling of data within their platform.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your project details remain confidential and are only used for the purpose of delivering our services to you.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality of the website.

7. Google Maps

We embed Google Maps on our website for location purposes. Google's privacy policy governs data collected through their mapping service.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
